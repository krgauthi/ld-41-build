Supremacy Slabs

About

`Our Game` was made for Ludum Dare 41 the theme was Combine 2 Incompatible Genres. We were inspired by the puzzle game 2048 and 4x/RTS games like Civ and age of empries.

How to Play:

  • Use your arrow keys to slide your units from side to side.
  • Gather Resources with your 3 Workers.
  • Protect your workers with your army.
  • If invadors take over a worker they will not be able to gather resources.
  • To free a captured Unit you can either combine with your army or upgrade that unit.
  • Invaders have a chance to get stronger every 50 turns.
  • For an Army to kill an Invader or help a captured worker they must be of equal or greater level.
  • You can level up your science for new abilities that can clear tiles from the board.
